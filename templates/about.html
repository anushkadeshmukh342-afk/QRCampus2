About PRPPEM

Four Decades of Engineering Excellence

Established in 1983, P R Pote Patil College of Engineering & Management has been a beacon of technical education in Vidarbha region, consistently producing skilled engineers and managers.

Our Vision

To be a premier institution of technical education, fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship while developing globally competent engineers and managers committed to sustainable development and social responsibility.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education through innovative teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art infrastructure, industry collaboration, and comprehensive personality development programs.
