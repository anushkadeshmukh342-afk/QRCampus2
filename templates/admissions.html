Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech Programs

  • 12th pass with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate (45% for reserved)
  • Valid JEE Main or State CET score
  • Age limit: 25 years (relaxation for reserved)

M.Tech Programs

  • B.Tech/B.E. in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in graduation
  • Valid GATE score preferred
  • Entrance test and interview

MBA Program

  • Graduate in any discipline
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks
  • Valid CAT/MAT/CMAT score
  • Group discussion and interview
